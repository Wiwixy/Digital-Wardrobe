Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our project

What is Kayseri 2050 project?

The Kayseri 2050 project aims to develop sustainable solutions for urban development, community engagement, and environmental protection in the city of Kayseri.

How can I participate?

You can join our initiatives by signing up on our website, attending workshops, and contributing to community projects.

Are the events free?

Most of our community events and online webinars are free of charge, ensuring everyone has access to participate.

How do you ensure sustainability?

We follow environmentally responsible practices, measure our carbon footprint, and engage with local partners to implement eco-friendly solutions.
